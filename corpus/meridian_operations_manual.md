# Meridian Funds — Operations Manual (Extract)

Document reference: MER-OPS-2025-07. This extract covers the operational, reporting, and record-keeping arrangements common to the Meridian fund range, with fund-specific facts noted where they differ. It is maintained by the operations team and reviewed annually.

## 1. Fund facts — Meridian Fixed Income Fund

The Meridian Fixed Income Fund was launched in 2018. The fund tracks the SGD Composite Bond Benchmark. Benchmark constituent data is licensed from the index provider and refreshed on each rebalancing date.

Performance is reported net of fees against the benchmark. Tracking commentary is included in the quarterly investor letter, which is distinct from the regulatory report described in Section 3.

## 2. Valuation and pricing

Portfolio instruments are valued using the pricing hierarchy in the valuation policy: traded prices, then evaluated prices, then model prices with documented inputs. Stale prices are flagged for the valuation committee.

The fund's net asset value is published daily. Publication follows the completion of the valuation control checks; a delayed publication is itself a recordable operational event.

## 3. Reporting obligations

The regulatory compliance report is produced quarterly. It states, for every limit in the investment guidelines, the current level, the limit, the utilisation, and any breach with its remediation status. The report is reviewed by compliance before submission.

Ad hoc reporting may be requested by the regulator or by the fund's trustees; such requests follow the same control path as the quarterly cycle.

## 4. Records and audit

Every compliance event, valuation exception, and report submission is recorded in the audit log with its supporting evidence. All audit records must be retained for seven years. Retention applies from the date of the event, and records under regulatory inquiry are held beyond the standard period until the inquiry closes.

Auditors are given read-only access to the audit log and may replay the evidence chain for any reported figure.

## 5. Service providers

Meridian Trust Services Pte Ltd acts as custodian of the fund. The custodian holds all fund assets in segregated accounts and provides the daily position feed used for compliance monitoring. Fund accounting and transfer agency are performed by the administrator named in the fund's offering documents.
